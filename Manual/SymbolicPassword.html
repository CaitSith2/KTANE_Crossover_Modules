On the Subject of Symbolic Passwords

Websites allow symbols in passwords now, but this is ridiculous!

  1. There are six symbols on the module. The expert's job is to find a 2x3 (2 high by 3 long) region in the table below with the same six symbols. THE SYMBOLS MAY NOT BE IN THE SAME ORDER (they will be mixed up in that 2x3 area).
  2. The defuser must use the buttons around the screen to rearrange the symbols to match the positions found in the corresponding 2x3 area in the table. The buttons have arrows next to them showing which way they move the symbols.
